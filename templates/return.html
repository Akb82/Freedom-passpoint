Troubleshooting

Not connecting automatically?

  • Make sure you're in range of a Freedom Wi-Fi hotspot
  • Check that WiFi is enabled on your device
  • Try forgetting and reconnecting to WiFi networks
  • Restart your device if issues persist

Want to remove the profile?

Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management and delete the profile.
